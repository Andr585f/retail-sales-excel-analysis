# Retail Sales Data Cleaning and Analysis

**Tools:** Microsoft Excel  
**Dataset:** Simulated retail orders dataset (800 rows, full year 2024)  
**Goal:** Clean a messy real-world style dataset and analyse sales performance

## What this project covers

- Data cleaning: removed duplicates, standardised date formats, fixed inconsistent 
  capitalisation, handled missing values
- Category analysis: revenue, cost, margin and order volume by product category
- Monthly trend analysis: revenue over time with month on month growth tracking
- Regional analysis: revenue and average order value broken down by region

## Key findings

- Electronics had the highest revenue and strongest profit margin across all categories
- Revenue peaked in Q1 and showed a gradual decline through mid-year
- The West region had the highest average order value despite fewer total orders
- 18 duplicate rows and 12 missing revenue entries were identified and handled during cleaning
